# PitchSide Pro: Revenue Performance & Growth Strategy Dashboard

## Overview

This project is an executive Power BI dashboard developed for the DataViz World Championship.

The dashboard analyzes five years of business data for PitchSide Pro, a fictional global football merchandise retailer, to identify the key drivers behind revenue performance and recommend strategies for sustainable growth.

---

## Business Question

**What is driving changes in revenue performance over time, and where should the business focus next to sustain growth?**

## Key Insights

- Regular Trading is the primary revenue driver.
- Football events create significant seasonal revenue peaks.
- EMEA is the strongest-performing region.
- Kits and Footwear are the highest-performing product categories.
- Digital channels continue to support long-term growth.
